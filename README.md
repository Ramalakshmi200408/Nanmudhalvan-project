ğŸ“ Abstract

The Calculating Family Expenses Using ServiceNow project aims to provide a digital solution to manage and monitor household income and expenses efficiently. The system helps families record their daily spending, analyze financial data, and identify areas where savings can be improved. By integrating with the ServiceNow platform, this project automates expense tracking, generates analytical reports, and promotes smart financial planning for better household budgeting.


ğŸ¯ Objectives

* To digitalize the process of recording and managing family income and expenses.
* To help families visualize their spending through interactive charts and reports.
* To automate expense categorization using ServiceNow workflows.
* To identify areas of overspending and support informed financial decisions.
* To promote a culture of financial awareness and savings among family members.

Income Management: Allows users to record various income sources and maintain monthly summaries.

Expense Management: Tracks daily and category-wise expenses such as food, rent, utilities, and education.

Reports & Analytics: Generates visual reports (like pie charts) for comparing income versus expenses.

User Dashboard: Displays a summarized overview of total income, total expenses, and savings in real-time.
